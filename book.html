Description

The Great Gatsby is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, first published in 1925. The story takes place in the summer of 1922 in the fictional town of West Egg, on Long Island. The novel is widely regarded as a classic of American literature and a masterpiece of the jazz age.

The story is narrated by Nick Carraway, a young man from the Midwest who moves to New York to work in the bond business. He becomes drawn into the glamorous and mysterious world of his neighbor, the wealthy Jay Gatsby, who throws lavish parties in hopes of winning back his lost love, Daisy Buchanan.
